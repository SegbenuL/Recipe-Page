Egusi Soup

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Food Image

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Egusi (ground melon seeds)
  • 1/4 cup Palm Oil
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger, minced
  • 2 cups Chicken or Beef Broth
  • 1 can Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Habanero Peppers, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon Curry Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Thyme
  • 1 bunch Spinach or Kale, chopped

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the palm oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions, garlic, and ginger and saut√© for 3-5 minutes, until softened.
  3. Add the Egusi and stir constantly for 5-7 minutes, until it starts to turn golden brown.
  4. Gradually add the chicken or beef broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
  5. Add the tomato sauce, Habanero peppers, salt, black pepper, curry powder, and thyme, and stir to combine.
  6. Reduce heat to low and let the soup simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add the chopped spinach or kale and stir to combine. Cook for an additional
    5-10 minutes until the greens are wilted and tender.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot with your choice of side dish.
